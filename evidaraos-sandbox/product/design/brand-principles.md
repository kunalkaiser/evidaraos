# EvidaraOS Brand Principles

EvidaraOS should feel like evidence infrastructure for life-sciences teams, not a generic AI wrapper.

## Core Impression

- precise
- governed
- clinical
- premium
- traceable
- validation-ready

## Product Promise

Turn biomedical literature, clinical evidence, and real-world evidence inputs into transparent workflows that expert teams can review, validate, audit, and report.

## Design Behavior

- Show the system, not only the slogan.
- Use diagrams, lifecycle rails, evidence matrices, audit surfaces, and product consoles.
- Prefer structured density over decorative space.
- Keep every claim conservative and source-aware.

## Never Do

- Do not imply clinical proof from AI output.
- Do not create unsupported metrics.
- Do not hide fixture-only status.
- Do not use generic SaaS cards as the main design language.
- Do not let one page drift into a different brand.
