# EvidaraOS Visual Reference Notes

This is a taste file, not a copying file. Do not clone another company's brand, layout, copy, or assets.

## Useful Reference Qualities

Linear-like qualities:

- precision
- restraint
- excellent spacing
- confident density

Stripe-like qualities:

- system diagrams
- infrastructure credibility
- strong information hierarchy

Enterprise health intelligence qualities:

- conservative claims
- trust and provenance
- clinical seriousness
- data lineage and governance

## EvidaraOS Translation

EvidaraOS should combine:

- dark evidence operations environment
- ivory and slate text system
- clinical teal structure lines
- sparse evidence-gold action states
- matrix and workflow visuals
- validation and audit surfaces

## Anti-References

Avoid:

- colorful startup gradients
- oversized emoji-style icons
- fake customer logo strips
- soft rounded pastel cards
- marketing pages that do not show how work happens
