# EvidaraOS Website IA

## Positioning

Evidara OS is a precision evidence operating system for life sciences teams.

Primary audience:

- Evidence generation teams
- HEOR teams
- Regulatory strategy teams
- Market access teams
- Medical affairs teams
- Evidence governance / quality teams

Core message:

Evidara OS helps evidence, HEOR, regulatory, and market access teams turn biomedical literature, clinical evidence, and real-world data into traceable, validation-ready evidence workflows.

- Establish enterprise life-sciences credibility.
- Explain EvidaraOS in one screen.
- Show modules as product capabilities, not generic DeerFlow skills.
- Drive demo requests.

1. Hero

4. Evidence workflow

Platform overview.

Narrative:

- EvidaraOS is a governed evidence intelligence layer.
- It connects question framing, biomedical retrieval, evidence extraction, human review, validation, and final reporting.
- DeerFlow powers the credited open-source agent runtime underneath, but the product UX is EvidaraOS.

## What Not To Show Publicly

- Raw DeerFlow skill names as product navigation.
- Generic “do anything” agent claims.
- Unsupported performance metrics.
- Fake live database counts.
- Demo artifacts as proof.
- “AI magic” language.
