## Interpretation

- Method agreement is a prioritization signal, not proof of efficacy.
- Conflicting signals must stay visible in the final review package.
- Missing method coverage should trigger false-negative gap checks.
- Every candidate remains hypothesis-only until reviewed and validated.

## Required Human Review

- Mechanistic directionality
- Clinical or preclinical evidence quality
- Safety transfer and contraindication concerns
- Population, dose, comparator, and endpoint fit
- Validation data availability
