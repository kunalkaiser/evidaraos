## Required Validation Steps

1. Verify primary evidence sources and study designs.
2. Review mechanism directionality and biological context.
3. Assess safety transfer, contraindications, and population-specific risks.
4. Run recall checks across missing method families.
5. Build a human-labeled benchmark before reporting performance.
6. Record expert decisions, rationale, and audit metadata.

## Language Controls

Allowed: hypothesis prioritized for review.

Disallowed: validated treatment, proven indication, clinical recommendation, or performance claim from fixture data.
