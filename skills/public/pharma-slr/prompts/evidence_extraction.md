# Evidence Extraction

Extract structured evidence from included studies.

Return:
- Study design
- Population and sample size
- Intervention or exposure
- Comparator
- Outcomes and timepoints
- Effect estimates or qualitative findings
- Safety findings and discontinuations
- Follow-up duration
- Limitations
- Funding or conflict notes when available
- DOI, PMID, and URL

If a field is absent from the abstract or full text, write "not reported" instead of inferring.
