# Title and Abstract Screening

Screen each normalized record against the protocol.

Classify each as:
- include
- exclude
- uncertain

Return a concise reason tied to inclusion/exclusion criteria. Preserve uncertainty when abstracts are missing or when full text is needed to confirm population, comparator, endpoint, design, or duplicate cohort overlap.

Use AI precision scores for prioritization only. Do not treat a low score as final exclusion when confidence is low, the abstract is missing, a sentinel paper is involved, or recall guardrails flag the record.
