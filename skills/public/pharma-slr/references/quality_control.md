# Quality Control

- Keep DeerFlow attribution and MIT license notices intact.
- Do not modify unrelated public skills while working on pharma-slr.
- Report exact source queries, dates, filters, and counts.
- Preserve source provenance during deduplication.
- Do not infer unavailable extraction fields; write "not reported".
- Clearly separate title/abstract screening from full-text eligibility.
- Label risk-of-bias outputs as placeholders unless a validated instrument was actually applied.
- Keep causal claims conservative and study-design appropriate.
- Verify every included study has a traceable PMID, DOI, or URL when available.
