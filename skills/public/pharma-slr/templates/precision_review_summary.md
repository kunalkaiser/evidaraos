## Limitations

- Search precision choices may miss poorly indexed or sparsely abstracted records.
- AI scoring supports prioritization but does not replace human inclusion decisions.
- Missing abstracts reduce confidence and should usually trigger human review.
- Citation chasing and sentinel validation should be completed before final exclusion in high-precision mode.
